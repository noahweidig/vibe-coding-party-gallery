WHO

Who is Vibe Coding For?

  • 🌿 Ecologists & Field Biologists: Turning messy R/Python scripts or data tables into interactive dashboards and maps without wrestling web syntax.
  • 💻 Computer Scientists & AI Researchers: Rapidly prototyping UIs, testing algorithm benchmarks, and polishing pseudocode into deployable tools.
  • 🌍 Citizen Scientists & Enthusiasts: Bringing creative conservation ideas and bioblitz tools to life with zero prior software background.
WHAT

What Can You Build?

  • Spatial & Occurence Visualizers: Web interfaces querying iNaturalist & GBIF APIs to display species ranges.
  • 🤖 ML Trait & Image Annotators: Tools using computer vision models (CLIP, YOLO) for automated camera trap or herbarium sorting.
  • 📊 Environmental Dashboards: Interactive telemetry tools tracking wildlife movements and sensor data.
WHY

Why Vibe Code?

  • ⚡ Accelerate Scientific Impact: Go from field idea to functional prototype in hours instead of months.
  • 🤝 Bridge Domain Silos: Enable biologists and computer scientists to collaborate directly using plain English.
  • 🌱 Open & Reproducible: Share working tools as open-source code for others to fork and build upon.
HOW

How to Get Involved

  • 🎉 Join Monthly Parties: Participate in our global virtual vibe coding sessions co-hosted by Karina, Kalindi, & Filippo.
  • 📜 Use Dan Morris' Template: Explore the foundational Vibe Coding Party Template.
  • 🚀 Submit Your Projects: Share your tools or resource recommendations to be featured in this hub!
